vaccination
for dogs & cats

Vaccinations protect your pet from infectious diseases, as some of them can be life-threatening for your dog or cat, have serious after-effects and, in the worst case, even result in the death of your beloved pet. Fortunately, it is possible to protect your pet against many of these diseases and infections with a vaccination - and you can do it easily at home.

Euthanasia
for dogs & cats

The decision to euthanize your dog or cat is not an easy one for any pet owner. But if the signs are clear and your animal is suffering more than necessary, it is time to take responsibility. If your dog wants to cross the rainbow bridge , you should be there for him and make the right decision together with the vet you trust. We must not let our animals suffer!

Grooming
for dogs & cats

Pet grooming will involve shaving, clipping, or trimming the coat when necessary. Some dogs & cats breeds require more trimming than others. Depending on the breed, the groomer will use special scissors and clippers to remove all unwanted hairs. Professional groomers have extensive knowledge about different dog & cat's breeds.
